DIPLOMA IN MEDICAL LABORATORY TECHNOLOGY(DMLT)
Duration Eligibility Affiliation
3 Years if joining after 10thstd + 3 months Internship 10thPass Paramedical Board Karnataka
2 Years if joining after 12thstd + 3 months Internship 12thPass(PCB) Paramedical Board Karnataka

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology DMLT is a medical Lab technologist course. It is a part of medical science responsible for performing various laboratory investigations relating to every diagnosis. It is the most vital area of investigations to the diagnosis of a disease. Laboratory technicians are prepared experts in charge of performing complex errands and methods that are therapeutic in nature. Their employment obligations incorporate gathering and breaking down body liquid and tissue tests for irregularities, and working research center hardware and other electronic apparatuses in a lab setting. Most lab specialists work in healing centers, however some work in veterinary facilities and research lab settings. The students become skilled to perform tests that aid in the diagnosis and treatment of that disease. The students are trained to collect samples, centrifuging, making slides and many other tests under proper guidance. The students are also taught the other vital parts of maintaining and recording results of tests and clinical records, evaluating and understanding the laboratory equipment and the latest test methodology.

In take capacity : 20 Seats

After completion of DMLT students can work in any government or private hospital or medical labs and there is no shortage of job opportunities in this field.
