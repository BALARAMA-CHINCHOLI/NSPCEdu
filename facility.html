Facility and Scope

Para medical courses in interdisciplinary medical subjects are becoming enormously important in Health Care Systems. In the recent years, there has been a growing need for qualified and well-trained Para Medical personnel. In order to meet the increasing demand for profession. In order to meet the increasing demand for qualified and trained para personnel it is necessary to impart the training to the eligible and interested students.

The manpower committee constituted by the government of Karnataka has emphasised that there is an acute shortage of paramedical personnel in our state. Taking this into consideration the state government has constitute this board in the year 1997 to conduct and to control the para medical courses.

The para medical board formed by the Government of Karnataka is having overall control over the government and private institutions running paramedical courses in the state.

The main functions of the board are to grant of affiliation, regulate admissions and control the functioning of institutions running paramedical courses with respect to imparting quality education program and conduct of examinations and award diploma certificate.

The successful candidates after the period of training will become eligible for appointment as technicians or assistants in various departments of Medical / Dental college hospitals, Primary Health Centres,CMC District hospitals and Clinical Laboratories at District and Taluk level, Nursing homes and also in other schemes of National Health Programs.The Paramedical courses provide great employment opportunities and also ensures the availability of sufficient number of qualified and trained paramedical personnel for the upliftment of a good healthcare system.
